The Dangers in My Heart, or abbreviated as BokuYaba, is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Norio Sakurai. It began serialization in Weekly Shōnen Champion in March 2018 and was moved to Champion Cross, before being moved again to Manga Cross. It has been published in 10 tankōbon volumes. Kyotaro Ichikawa is a reclusive teenager who desires to murder his popular classmate, Anna Yamada. Circumstances begin to change when his feelings develop into something else.
